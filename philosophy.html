🔍 How I Invest
How I Invest
Not a strategy to copy. Not financial advice. Just a transparent look at how one real investor thinks — so you can decide if it resonates.
✔ Long-term mindset (5–10+ years) ✔ Focus on business, not charts ✔ Covered calls for income ✔ Actively managed — not passive
01 — Philosophy
🕐
Long-term mindset — not a fixed timeline
Ideally 5–10+ years. But long-term mindset ≠ guaranteed holding period. If the thesis changes, valuation stretches, or better opportunities arise, I may exit earlier. That’s part of actively managing a real portfolio.
🔍
A dip isn’t a reason to buy — the business is
I focus on revenue and user growth, expansion potential, path to profitability, and long-term narrative. Price follows business execution over time — not short-term price action.
💰
Options income compounds returns
I sell covered calls to generate income, reinvested back into the portfolio. Reduces cost basis, accelerates compounding — an extension of long-term holding, not a separate strategy.
😐
Red months don’t change the plan
I’ve held through multiple 30–50% drawdowns. I post more during red markets, not less. This isn’t about showing green months — it’s about showing the full process.
02 — Reality
⚖️
Not every position works
I’m not right 100% of the time. Some theses break, some positions underperform, and I may exit early. I share those decisions transparently — with full reasoning. I only need a few to work really well.
⚠️
Results will differ
Members may not enter or exit at the same prices or times as me. Every member makes their own independent decisions. Nothing shared should be interpreted as instructions to act.
03 — Research Process
🔎
How ideas are formed
I study multiple sources — investor commentary, earnings calls, and industry insights. Ideas can come from anywhere. Conviction, position sizing, and execution are built independently.
